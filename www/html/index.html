About Lunarium

  • 100% Proof of Stake 3.0 Consensus protocol, allowing very low transaction fees and energy expenditure, and staking rewards to all participants in the network.
  • Masternode technology used to secure the network and provide additional features. Each Masternode is secured with collateral of 10,000 XLN.
  • Decentralized blockchain voting utilizing Masternode technology. The blockchain will distribute monthly treasury funds based on successful proposals submitted by the community and voted on by the Masternodes.

Features

Stable Network

Lunarium Masternodes ensure the stability of the network. The Lunarium protocol is developed with stability, and robustness in mind.

Privacy

Transactions are always anonymous, and processed on our decentralized network.

Fast Transactions

The Lunarium blockchain is supported by a growing network of Masternodes, that guarantees that your transactions are processed rapidly.

Rewards

Owners of Masternode are rewarded with XLN as long as they run their masternode. Masternode rewards are great source of passive income.

Best Wallet

Instead of implementing every features found in other wallets, we instead empower users with an easy-to-use, and a stable interface.

And More to Come!
